clothing recycling

Do you ever find clothing in your father,mother, older brother or sister wardrobes. You really like the patern, style or the fabric. You can transform those items and make them yours. Al you need is:

  1. Needles
  2. Pins
  3. Threads
  4. A idea
  5. Old clothing 👗
  6. (if you have a sewing machine. Use it to make your life a lot easier.)

You can make pants smaller. how? put on the pants inside out and look how much you need to cut off. Put the pins a little further away from your leg, so you can take the pants of. now sow where the pins are en cut offleft over fabrics buyt at least half a centimeter away from the part you just sowed.
